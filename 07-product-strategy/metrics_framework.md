# Product metrics framework

## North-star decision metric

**Weighted complaint severity score** = normalized topic volume × average negative sentiment intensity × commercial exposure weight. The prioritization engine supplements this with negative share and RICE to avoid allowing any single proxy to dictate the roadmap.

## Guardrails

- Median support resolution time must not worsen after routing changes.
- Topic-tag precision should exceed 80% on a stratified, manually reviewed sample.
- At least 90% of high-impact recommendations must include five inspectable verbatims.
- Track complaint rate per active customer when denominator data becomes available; raw review counts can reflect usage growth.
- Monitor sentiment and topic performance by source/platform to detect systematic classification bias.

## So what?

The framework rewards large, severe issues while preventing automation quality or support outcomes from becoming hidden costs.
